How to Play

Welcome to the world with four directions of gravity! 4Gravity is a multiplayer game. 3-4 players are recommended. First player to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four tiles to win. You can only put a tile that is supported by at least one side of the grid. Tap on a tile to make a move!

Tips:
  • Turn on Learning Mode to view the available moves!
  • If you have a false move, tap on the "Retract" button to retract your move.
